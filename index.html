What We Do

EXPERT ELECTRICAL
SERVICES

01
Emergency Electrical
Power out? Sparking outlet? We respond 24/7 with fully stocked vans ready to diagnose and fix the problem fast.

03
Lighting Installation

04
Whole-Home Rewiring
Aluminum wiring, outdated systems, renovation projects — our team rewires homes cleanly and efficiently with minimal disruption.
05

06
Surge Protection

07
Smart Home Wiring
Smart lighting, automated systems, and custom wiring for the modern connected home — installed clean and configured right.
08
Electrical Inspections
Buying a home or unsure about your wiring? Our detailed inspections catch issues early and give you confidence and peace of mind.
09
Generator Installation
Oklahoma weather demands backup power. We install whole-home generator systems sized exactly for your needs.
